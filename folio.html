The first human-friendly password manager.

Tassu Passu's solution for human-friendly password manager is a password made of pictures, connected using a story. It is fun, creative and secure. Try out Tassu Passu demo with your mobile. You will create your Tassu Passu Story which you will soon be able to use to log in to different websites.
